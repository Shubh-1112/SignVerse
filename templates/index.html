FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Check out our most frequently asked questions below.

What makes SignVerse different from other platforms?

Unlike regular video calling apps, SignVerse is built with accessibility first. It offers ISL-focused translation, gamified learning, emotional design, and inclusivity-driven features that connect people beyond just words.

Is SignVerse free, or do I need a subscription?

SignVerse is free for everyone. Our goal is to help people learn, communicate, and connect with the deaf and hard-of-hearing community without barriers. We believe accessibility and understanding should never come with a price tag.

How secure is my data?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations and translations on SignVerse are protected with end-to-end encryption, meaning only you and the person you’re talking to can access them. We never store your video calls, and any text or sign data used for translation stays secure and confidential.

Can I practice signs even if I don’t have anyone to talk to right now?

Yes! The solo practice mode lets you play interactive games, quizzes, and challenges so you can learn ISL anytime, anywhere — no partner required.

Do you provide customer support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 customer support through various channels including live chat, email, and phone. Our support team is always ready to help you.
